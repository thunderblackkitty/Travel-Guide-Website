Bonus Places Nearby Winschoten

Bourtange Fortress

Location: 30 min from Winschoten

About the place: A star-shaped 16th-century fortress village,
perfectly preserved.

Opening Hours: Open Daily

What you can do:

  • Walk through historic streets.
  • Visit museums.
  • Watch reenactments.

Entry fee: €5–€10

Tips:

  • One of the BEST attractions nearby.
  • Go early to avoid crowds.

Groninger Museum

Location: Groningen city

About the place: A famous modern museum known for art, design, and unique architecture.

Opening Hours: Open daily between 10:00-17:00 expect Monday

What you can do:

  • Explore exhibitions.
  • See contemporary & classic art.
  • Photography.

Entry fee: €15 adults

Tips:

  • Combine with Groningen city trip.
  • Exterior is very Instagram-worthy.

Winschoterdiep Canal

Location: Runs through Winschoten

About the place: Historic canal once used for trade and transport.

Opening Hours: Always Open

What you can do:

  • Walk or cycle along the water.
  • Enjoy views & photography.

Entry fee: Free

Tips:

  • Nice for sunset walks.

Roos van Toos (Art Sculpture)

Location: City center

About the place: A modern sculpture symbolizing local culture and identity.

Opening Hours: Always visible

What you can do:

  • Take photos.
  • Learn local story.

Entry fee: Free

Tips:

  • Quick stop while exploring town.

Reiderwolde Toren & Amphitheater

Location: Near Blauwestad

About the place: A scenic tower and open-air theater in a nature area.

Opening Hours: Daytime access

What you can do:

  • Climb tower for views.
  • Relax at amphitheater.

Entry fee: Free

Tips:

  • Great for nature lovers.

Herrenhuus Museum

Location: Midwolda near Winschoten

About the place: Museum inside a historic manor showing regional history & lifestyle.

Opening Hours: Seasonal / limited

What you can do:

  • Explore exhibitions.
  • Learn local heritage.

Entry fee: €5

Tips:

  • Combine with Blauwestad visit.

Winschoten Zoo

Location: Winschoten

About the place: A small local zoo with animals and green space.

Opening Hours: Open Daily

What you can do:

  • Family outing.
  • See animals.

Entry fee: Low-cost / varies

Tips:

  • Good for kids.

Langestraat (Shopping Street)

Location: City center

About the place: Main shopping street with stores, cafés, and boutiques.

Opening Hours: 09:00–18:00

What you can do:

  • Shopping.
  • Eat & drink.

Entry fee: Free

Tips:

  • Busy on weekends.

Blauwestad

Location: Near Winschoten

About the place: Modern lake area with water sports and nature.

Opening Hours: Always Open

What you can do:

  • Swim, boat.
  • Relax.

Entry fee: Free

Tips:

  • Best in summer.

Heiligerlee Village

Location: 10 min from Winschoten

About the place: Historic village known for the Battle of Heiligerlee (1568).

Opening Hours: Always Open

What you can do:

  • Visit monuments.
  • Walk around village.

Entry fee: Free

Tips:

  • Good for history lovers.
